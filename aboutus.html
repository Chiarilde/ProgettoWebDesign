ABOUT GLERAS WINES

Nestled in the heart of rolling vineyards, Glera Winery is a haven for lovers of fine wine and breathtaking landscapes. With a history rooted in tradition, the winery is named after the Glera grape, the signature variety of the region, known for producing crisp, aromatic wines.

At the heart of Glera Winery is its passionate owner Corbella, a young visionary whose love for winemaking is evident in every aspect of the estate. Her dedication is reflected in every bottle, where quality and sustainability are the top priorities.

At Glera Winery, every bottle tells a story. The vineyard is carefully cultivated using sustainable practices, ensuring the finest quality grapes for every harvest. From refreshing sparkling wines to full-bodied reds, Glera’s collection is a testament to the region’s rich winemaking heritage.
